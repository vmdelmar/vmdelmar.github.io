Welcome to My Final Project page!





My name is Vanessa DelMar and I am SO happy to be done with this class! HAHA. Joking! (not really). My final project is basically an education game for children. As a mom, I've noticed that most of the apps for children are mostly teaching colors, shapes and geographical things of sorts. I thought it would be cool to create a matching game that taught different types of antonyms. I originally wanted to add way more antonym options than I had available but since I am just not computer saavy enough, I settled for the pairs I had.

The game is pretty simple in nature. It's essentially finding the correct matching antonym.
To make it more challenging: try and get it under 30 attempts or under 120 seconds.
